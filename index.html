About Me

Who I am and how I think about learning.

I am Amit Kumar Sankhyan, a Training & Development Professional from Himachal Pradesh, India. I help individuals and teams grow through learning — not with theory alone, but with real-world application.

My journey from customer service floors to corporate training rooms has taught me discipline, empathy, and the power of clear communication. I believe training should be practical, simple, and emotionally real.

In my work I combine structured methodologies like ADDIE, Kirkpatrick, and 70:20:10 with storytelling and coaching to create sessions that are memorable and measurable.

What I Believe

  • Training is not about slides; it is about changing people.
  • Every session must create value for both learners and business.
  • Every learner deserves to feel seen, heard, and respected.
